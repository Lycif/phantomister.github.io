Data Deletion Instructions

If you would like your data to be deleted, please follow the steps below:

  • Send an email request to renorsi@gmail.com with the subject line “Data Deletion Request”.
  • Include your Messenger, Instagram, or WhatsApp account details used to interact with the chatbot.
  • We will delete all data related to your account within 7 business days.

For urgent matters, please contact us directly at the same email.
